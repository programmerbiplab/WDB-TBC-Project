Welcome to World Hotels

Since our establishment, World Hotels has been committed to providing exceptional hospitality experiences across 17 beautiful UK cities. We believe that every journey deserves a comfortable home away from home.

Our Story

World Hotels began with a simple vision: to create a network of welcoming, comfortable hotels across the United Kingdom's most vibrant cities. From our first property to our current 17 locations, we've maintained our commitment to quality, service, and guest satisfaction.

Each of our hotels reflects the unique character of its city while maintaining the consistent standards of comfort and service our guests have come to expect.
